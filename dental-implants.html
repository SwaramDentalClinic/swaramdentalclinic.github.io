What Are Dental Implants?

The Permanent Solution for Missing Teeth

A dental implant is a small titanium post surgically placed into the jawbone to replace the root of a missing tooth. Once it fuses with the bone (osseointegration), a custom-made dental crown is attached — giving you a replacement tooth that looks, feels, and functions exactly like a natural one.

At Swaram Dental Clinic in Hinjewadi, every implant case is planned by Dr. Swati Shivane using 3D CBCT imaging and digital treatment planning, and placed by our experienced MDS-qualified implant specialist. This ensures precise placement, optimal aesthetics, and long-term success. Whether you are missing one tooth, several teeth, or all teeth, we have an implant solution for you.

Why implants over other options: Unlike bridges that file down healthy neighbouring teeth, or dentures that slip and require adhesive, implants are the only tooth replacement that preserves your jawbone, stays permanently fixed, and requires no special care beyond regular brushing and flossing.

Types of Dental Implants We Offer

From a single missing tooth to a full-mouth restoration — Swaram Dental Clinic has the right implant solution for you.

🦷

Single Tooth Implant

One titanium implant post with a custom zirconia or ceramic crown. The most natural-looking and permanent solution for a single missing tooth. No impact on adjacent teeth.

🦷🦷

Multiple Teeth Implants

Two or more implants to replace adjacent missing teeth using individual crowns or an implant-supported bridge. Stronger and more hygienic than a conventional bridge.

Full-Mouth Implants

All-on-4 or All-on-6 implant-supported dentures replace an entire arch of teeth with just 4 or 6 implants. Fixed, non-removable, and dramatically better than traditional dentures.

🔩

Implant-Supported Crown & Bridge

Implants act as anchors for a dental bridge — eliminating the need to grind down healthy adjacent teeth, unlike a conventional bridge.

Why Choose Implants

Benefits of Dental Implants

Dental implants are the closest thing to your natural teeth. Here's why they are the gold standard for tooth replacement.

Looks & Feels Natural

Custom-designed zirconia crowns match the colour, shape, and size of your natural teeth perfectly. Nobody can tell it's an implant.

Permanent & Fixed

Unlike dentures, implants are permanently anchored in the bone. No slipping, no adhesives, no removal at night — just confidence every day.

Preserves Jawbone

The titanium post stimulates the jawbone just like a natural tooth root. This prevents the bone loss and facial collapse that occurs after tooth extraction.

Restores Full Function

Eat anything you want — apples, nuts, hard foods — with full biting force. Speak clearly without the lisping that can occur with dentures.

No Impact on Adjacent Teeth

Unlike a bridge, implants don't require grinding down neighbouring healthy teeth. Each implant stands independently, preserving your natural dentition.

Cost-Effective Long Term

Implants last 20+ years with proper care. When you factor in the lifetime cost of replacing dentures and bridges every 5–10 years, implants are the most economical choice.

Step by Step

The Dental Implant Process at Swaram Dental Clinic

Every implant case is carefully planned and executed for the best possible outcome. Here's what to expect.

  1. 01

    Consultation & 3D CBCT Scan

    Dr. Swati Shivane conducts a thorough consultation, evaluates your dental health, and arranges a 3D CBCT scan to assess bone volume, density, and anatomy. A detailed digital treatment plan is created.

  2. 02

    Treatment Planning & Implant Selection

    Our specialist team maps the ideal implant size, position, and angulation digitally. Crown design is planned to match your natural teeth precisely in colour, shape, and size.

  3. 03

    Implant Placement (Surgery)

    Under local anaesthesia, our experienced implant specialist precisely places the titanium implant post into the jawbone. The procedure is comfortable and typically takes 30–60 minutes per implant.

  4. 04

    Osseointegration — Healing Period

    The implant fuses naturally with the jawbone over 2–4 months. This osseointegration gives the implant the strength and stability of a natural tooth root. A temporary crown may be provided during this period.

  5. 05

    Abutment Placement

    Once osseointegration is confirmed, our specialist attaches a small connector piece (abutment) to the implant post to hold the final crown securely.

  6. 06

    Custom Crown Fitting

    A custom zirconia or ceramic crown — matched precisely to your adjacent teeth — is permanently attached. Your new tooth is complete, looking and functioning exactly like a natural tooth.

After Your Implant

Recovery & Aftercare for Dental Implants

Most patients resume normal activities the next day. Mild soreness and swelling for 3–5 days is normal and easily managed with prescribed medication.

What to Expect After Surgery

  • Mild soreness and swelling for 3–5 days — normal healing response
  • Some bruising on the gum or cheek may appear — settles within a week
  • Eat soft foods for the first 1–2 weeks
  • Avoid smoking — it significantly reduces implant success rates
  • Keep the area clean with gentle rinsing as directed

Long-Term Implant Care

  • Brush twice daily and floss around the implant crown
  • Use an interdental brush to clean around the implant
  • Schedule professional cleaning every 6 months
  • Avoid chewing ice or very hard objects
  • Report any pain, mobility, or gum changes promptly
Implants vs Alternatives

How Implants Compare

Understanding why implants are superior to other tooth replacement options helps you make the right long-term decision.

Implants vs Dentures

Dentures are removable and can slip during eating or speaking. Implants are fixed permanently — no adhesive, no embarrassment, and they preserve jawbone which dentures cannot.

Implants vs Dental Bridge

A bridge requires grinding down the two healthy teeth on either side of the gap. An implant is standalone — adjacent teeth remain fully intact. Implants also last far longer.
