Meet Your Dentist

Dr. Swati Shivane

A highly experienced dental specialist holding an MDS in Endodontics & Conservative Dentistry — Dr. Swati Shivane is among Pune's most trusted root canal experts and the founder of Swaram Dental Clinic, Hinjewadi.

Dr. Swati Shivane completed her post-graduation with an MDS in Endodontics and Conservative Dentistry, specialising in the diagnosis and treatment of diseases of the dental pulp and surrounding tissues. Her clinical focus is root canal treatment, restorative dentistry, and comprehensive aesthetic care.

As the founder of Swaram Dental Clinic, she has built a practice rooted in clinical excellence, ethical care, and patient comfort. Under her leadership, the clinic has grown into one of Hinjewadi's most respected dental practices — serving thousands of patients from Hinjewadi, Maan, Mahalunge, Marunji, Wakad, Baner, and across Pune.

Dr. Shivane believes that every patient deserves honest, personalised care — not rushed appointments or unnecessary treatments. Her approach is evidence-based, compassionate, and focused on long-term oral health rather than short-term fixes.

She is equally comfortable performing a painless root canal, placing a dental implant, designing a complete smile transformation, or gently treating a nervous child's first filling. That breadth of expertise is what makes Swaram Dental Clinic a true one-stop dental destination for the entire family.

MDS – Endodontics & Conservative Dentistry

BDS – Bachelor of Dental Surgery

Cosmetic & Aesthetic Dentistry

Continuous Professional Development
