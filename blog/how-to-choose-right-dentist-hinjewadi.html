Why Swaram Dental Clinic Is the Right Choice for Every Dental Treatment in Hinjewadi

Hinjewadi has no shortage of dental clinics. What is rare is a clinic where every treatment — from a routine checkup to a full smile makeover, from a root canal to a dental implant — is handled by the right expert with the right training and the right technology. That is what Swaram Dental Clinic delivers, and this article explains exactly how.

SS

Dr. Swati Shivane

BDS, MDS – Endodontics & Conservative Dentistry | Swaram Dental Clinic, Hinjewadi

One Clinic. Expert Care for Every Treatment.

Most patients in Hinjewadi, Blue Ridge Township, Maan and Marunji face the same dilemma — finding a single clinic that covers every dental need without having to travel across the city for different specialists. Swaram Dental Clinic was built to solve exactly that problem.

Led by Dr. Swati Shivane and supported by a team of MDS-qualified specialists, the clinic provides complete dental care — preventive, restorative, cosmetic, surgical and emergency — all under one roof. Here is how every treatment is covered.

Root Canal Treatment — Led by a Specialist

Root canal treatment at Swaram Dental Clinic is performed by Dr. Swati Shivane herself, who holds an MDS in Endodontics & Conservative Dentistry — the postgraduate specialisation specifically for root canal care. This is not a general dentist doing root canals on the side; this is the specialist, treating you directly.

Dr. Swati uses rotary nickel-titanium instruments for precision and comfort, an electronic apex locator to measure root lengths accurately, and digital X-rays for instant high-resolution imaging. Complex cases — curved canals, calcified canals, re-treatment of failed root canals — fall squarely within her core training. Patients who have had root canals at Swaram Dental describe the procedure as far more comfortable than they expected, often completing it in a single visit.

Learn more about root canal treatment at Swaram Dental Clinic.

Root Canal Treatment

Performed directly by Dr. Swati Shivane, MDS – Endodontics & Conservative Dentistry. Rotary instruments, apex locator, digital X-ray. Single-visit RCT available for eligible cases.

Cosmetic Dentistry & Smile Makeovers — Dr. Swati's Artistic Precision

Every cosmetic treatment at Swaram Dental Clinic is performed personally by Dr. Swati Shivane. Her combination of clinical skill and an eye for facial aesthetics has made her the go-to cosmetic dentist for patients across Hinjewadi and beyond.

Teeth Whitening

Dr. Swati uses a professional light-activated whitening system with careful gum barrier protection. The result is a significant, natural-looking shade improvement — not the patchy or sensitivity-heavy experience patients sometimes get from less careful providers. Custom take-home trays are provided to maintain the result long-term.

Smile Makeovers

A smile makeover at Swaram Dental is entirely personalised. Dr. Swati photographs your smile, analyses your facial proportions and lip line, and designs a treatment plan specific to your goals. Where appropriate she creates a wax-up model so you can preview the outcome before any treatment begins. The final result — whether composite bonding, gum contouring, veneers, or a combination — consistently looks like a natural enhancement of your own smile, not an obvious cosmetic procedure.

Veneers

Dr. Swati offers both porcelain and composite veneers with conservative preparation to preserve as much natural tooth structure as possible. The ceramic lab she works with produces veneers with natural translucency and depth that composite alone cannot replicate. Patients frequently remark that their veneers look like teeth they were simply born with.

Explore smile designing at Swaram Dental.  |  Learn about professional teeth whitening.

Cosmetic Dentistry

All cosmetic treatments — whitening, veneers, bonding, gum contouring, smile makeovers, clear aligners — performed directly by Dr. Swati Shivane.

Dental Implants — Specialist Surgical Team, Dr. Swati's Oversight

Implant surgery at Swaram Dental Clinic is performed by the clinic's experienced MDS-qualified specialist team. Dr. Swati leads every implant case — from the first consultation and CBCT-based 3D treatment planning right through to the final crown placement and follow-up reviews. Her oversight ensures the entire process is clinically sound and the outcome meets the standard her patients expect.

Whether you need a single implant, multiple implants, or implant-supported dentures, the approach at Swaram Dental is thorough: comprehensive assessment, realistic treatment planning, surgical precision, and long-term monitoring. Patients from Hinjewadi, Maan and Marunji no longer need to travel to a separate specialist clinic. Find out more about dental implants at Swaram Dental.

Dental Implants

Implant surgery by our MDS-qualified specialist team. Consultation, 3D treatment planning, and aftercare led by Dr. Swati Shivane. CBCT-guided placement for accuracy.

Wisdom Tooth Care — Expert Assessment and Safe Removal

Wisdom tooth cases involve more complexity than most patients realise — nerve proximity, bone density, angulation, risk to adjacent teeth. At Swaram Dental, Dr. Swati performs a detailed OPG and CBCT assessment for every wisdom tooth case, evaluates the risks carefully, and determines the right approach before recommending removal. The surgical extraction is carried out by our experienced MDS-qualified specialist team.

The result is that patients across Hinjewadi and Wakad get expert assessment, clear clinical reasoning for the decision, and professional surgical care — not a rushed extraction at a general clinic. Read about wisdom tooth removal at Swaram Dental.

Children's Dentistry — Confidence from the Very First Visit

Dr. Swati and the Swaram Dental team have treated children from toddlers to teenagers, and have built a well-earned reputation for making young patients feel safe. The approach is consistent: take the time to build trust, introduce instruments gently, explain everything, and never proceed until the child is comfortable. Families from Blue Ridge Township, Mahalunge and Kaspate Wasti whose children had anxiety at previous clinics frequently find that just two or three visits at Swaram Dental changes everything — children who refused to open their mouths start asking when they can come back.

Dr. Swati also monitors developmental patterns at every visit — catching early orthodontic concerns, space management issues, and signs of grinding in time for preventive intervention. Learn about children's dentistry at Swaram Dental.

Emergency Dental Care — Open When You Need It

Dental emergencies do not wait for convenient hours. Swaram Dental Clinic is open 10:00 AM to 9:00 PM, seven days a week, which means same-day access is available whether you develop an abscess on a Saturday, crack a tooth on a Sunday evening, or lose a crown during a working week. Dr. Swati stabilises emergency cases with the same care as planned appointments — rapid pain relief, clear explanation of what happened, and a follow-up plan sorted before you leave. Find out about emergency dental care at Swaram Dental.

The Technology Behind the Care

Good dentistry requires good tools. Swaram Dental Clinic is equipped with the technology that allows Dr. Swati and the specialist team to diagnose accurately and treat precisely:

  • Digital X-rays — instant high-resolution images reviewed with patients on screen; lower radiation than traditional film
  • Intraoral camera — patients see inside their own mouths in real time, making findings transparent and understandable
  • CBCT / 3D imaging — used for implant planning and complex root canal anatomy; the gold standard for pre-surgical assessment
  • Rotary endodontic system — faster, more precise, more comfortable root canal treatment
  • Electronic apex locator — accurate root length measurement, critical for root canal success
