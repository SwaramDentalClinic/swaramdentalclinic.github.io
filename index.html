Common Questions About Dental Care

Answers to the questions patients ask us most often.

Modern root canal treatment at Swaram Dental Clinic is virtually painless. Dr. Swati Shivane uses advanced anaesthesia protocols and rotary endodontic instruments that significantly reduce procedure time and discomfort. Most patients are surprised at how comfortable the experience actually is.
With proper care, dental implants can last a lifetime. The titanium implant post permanently fuses to your jawbone, while the crown typically lasts 15–25 years. Regular dental check-ups and good oral hygiene are key to implant longevity.
Clear aligners work well for mild to moderate dental misalignment — crowding, spacing, and bite issues. They're ideal for adults and teenagers who want a discreet orthodontic option. During your consultation, Dr. Swati Shivane will evaluate whether aligners are right for your specific case.
Children should visit a dentist when their first tooth appears — usually around 6 months — or no later than their first birthday. Early visits help establish good oral health habits, identify potential issues early, and make children comfortable with dental care as they grow.
Yes. Swaram Dental Clinic provides same-day emergency dental care for toothaches, broken teeth, lost fillings, and dental trauma. Call +91 84848 51686 immediately and we will accommodate you as quickly as possible. We're open Monday to Sunday, 10 AM – 9 PM.
A professional dental cleaning every 6 months is recommended for most patients. If you have gum disease, a history of tartar build-up, or other risk factors, your dentist may recommend more frequent visits — every 3 to 4 months — to maintain optimal oral health.
