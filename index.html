What Are Dental Implants and Why Are They the Best Choice?

Dental implants are artificial tooth roots made of biocompatible materials like titanium. They are surgically placed into the jawbone to support replacement teeth or bridges, offering a permanent solution for missing teeth. Unlike dentures or bridges, dental implants are fixed in place, providing a stable and secure foundation that looks and feels just like natural teeth.

Key Benefits of Dental Implants:

  • Durability: Dental implants are designed to last for decades, providing long-term benefits.
  • Natural Appearance: Implants blend seamlessly with your natural teeth, offering a realistic and aesthetically pleasing smile.
  • Improved Functionality: With dental implants, you can chew and speak with confidence without the discomfort or inconvenience of removable dentures.
  • Bone Preservation: Unlike other tooth replacement options, dental implants help preserve your jawbone, preventing bone loss.

The Dental Implant Procedure – What to Expect

At Swaram Dental Clinic, we take the time to explain every step of the dental implant process so that you feel comfortable and well-informed.

Step 1: Consultation and Planning

The process begins with a comprehensive consultation. Dr. Swati Shivane will assess your oral health, discuss your goals, and create a personalized treatment plan. We use digital impressions and 3D imaging to plan the procedure with precision.

Step 2: Implant Placement

The dental implant is surgically placed into the jawbone. This process typically takes one to two hours, depending on the complexity of the case. We use local anesthesia or sedation to ensure that you are relaxed and pain-free throughout the procedure.

Step 3: Healing and Osseointegration

Once the implant is placed, it requires a few months to fuse with the jawbone in a process called osseointegration. This ensures that the implant becomes a permanent part of your jaw.

Step 4: Abutment and Crown Placement

After the healing period, an abutment is placed on top of the implant, and a custom-made crown is attached to restore the function and aesthetics of your tooth. The final result is a durable and natural-looking tooth that blends seamlessly with your existing smile.
