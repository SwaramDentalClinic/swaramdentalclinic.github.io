What is Full Mouth Rehabilitation?

Full mouth rehabilitation (also called full mouth reconstruction) is a customised combination of dental treatments designed to restore the function, health and appearance of all your teeth. Unlike a single treatment, it addresses the complete picture — worn teeth, missing teeth, gum disease, bite problems, decay, and aesthetics — in one coordinated plan.

Dr. Swati Shivane works with our MDS-qualified specialist team — oral surgeon, periodontist, and prosthodontist — to plan and execute every stage at Swaram Dental Clinic, Hinjewadi.

Who Needs Full Mouth Rehabilitation?

  • Multiple missing teeth requiring implants or bridges
  • Severely worn or fractured teeth from grinding (bruxism)
  • Advanced gum disease affecting multiple teeth
  • Old or failing dental work needing replacement
  • Trauma or accident causing damage to several teeth
  • Patients wanting a complete smile transformation
