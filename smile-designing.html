What is Smile Designing?

Your Dream Smile, Designed Just for You

Smile designing — also called a smile makeover — is a comprehensive cosmetic dental treatment that combines multiple procedures to improve the appearance of your smile. It addresses the colour, shape, size, alignment, and proportion of your teeth, as well as your gum line, to create a smile that is natural, beautiful, and uniquely yours.

At Swaram Dental Clinic in Hinjewadi, Dr. Swati Shivane uses a digital smile design process — your new smile is previewed on screen before any treatment begins. You see exactly what you'll look like, and we refine it together until it's perfect. Every smile makeover is fully personalised to your face shape, skin tone, and aesthetic goals.

Smile designing is not one treatment — it's a plan. Depending on your needs, it may include veneers, composite bonding, teeth whitening, gum contouring, crowns, clear aligners, or a combination. Dr. Swati Shivane will recommend only what genuinely improves your smile.

What's Included

Treatments We Combine in a Smile Makeover

A smile makeover at Swaram Dental Clinic is fully customised. Your plan may include any combination of the following treatments.

✨

Porcelain Veneers

Ultra-thin porcelain shells bonded to the front of teeth to correct colour, shape, chips, gaps, and mild misalignment. Highly durable, stain-resistant, and completely natural looking.

🎨

Composite Bonding

Tooth-coloured composite resin sculpted directly on the tooth to fix chips, gaps, stains, and irregular shapes. Minimally invasive, completed in a single visit, and reversible.

⚪

Teeth Whitening

Professional in-office whitening to brighten your smile by several shades. Faster and more effective than over-the-counter products, with results that last months to years.

🌿

Gum Contouring

Reshaping the gum line to correct a "gummy smile" or uneven gums. Reveals more of the tooth surface for a balanced, proportionate smile frame.

👑

Zirconia Crowns

For heavily damaged, discoloured, or misshapen teeth, a zirconia crown provides full coverage with exceptional aesthetics and long-term durability.

🔲

Clear Aligners

When mild crowding or spacing affects your smile, clear aligners can straighten teeth invisibly before veneer or bonding treatment — enhancing the final result.

Who Is It For?

Smile Concerns We Correct

If any of these describe your smile, a consultation with Dr. Swati Shivane at Swaram Dental Clinic, Hinjewadi can show you exactly what's possible.

  • Stained or discoloured teeth — yellowing, greyness, or dark stains that whitening alone cannot fix
  • Chipped or cracked teeth — visible damage affecting the appearance of front teeth
  • Gaps between teeth — diastema or spacing that creates an uneven smile
  • Uneven or irregular tooth shape — teeth that are too short, too long, or asymmetrical
  • Gummy smile — excess gum tissue making teeth appear small or uneven
  • Mild crowding or misalignment — teeth that are slightly crooked affecting overall smile aesthetics

Most Popular Option

Porcelain Veneers vs Composite Bonding — Which Is Right for You?

The two most common smile designing treatments — understanding the difference helps you choose the right path.

✨

Porcelain Veneers

Best for: Severe staining, multiple shape corrections, long-lasting results.
Durability: 10–15 years.
Process: 2 visits — prep and bonding.
Appearance: Most natural, most stain-resistant.

🎨

Composite Veneers

Best for: Minor corrections, budget-conscious patients, reversible option.
Durability: 5–7 years.
Process: Single visit, done directly on the tooth.
Appearance: Excellent when done by a skilled specialist.

💻

Digital Smile Design

Both options begin with digital smile design — so you see your result before any tooth is touched. Dr. Swati Shivane will recommend the best option based on your specific teeth and goals.

⚪

Teeth Whitening

Best for: Yellow or stained teeth that are otherwise healthy in shape.
Durability: 1–2 years with touch-ups.
Process: Single in-office session, 10–20 minutes.
Result: Up to 3 shades whiter.

🌿

Gum Contouring

Best for: Gummy smiles, uneven gum lines, teeth that look too short.
Process: Laser or scalpel reshaping, single visit.
Recovery: 1–2 days mild tenderness.
Result: Permanent gum reshaping.

👑

Zirconia Crowns

Best for: Heavily damaged or discoloured teeth needing full coverage.
Durability: 15–20 years.
Appearance: Metal-free, translucent, completely natural.
Pairs well with: Veneers on adjacent teeth.
