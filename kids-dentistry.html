Why Milk Teeth Matter

Milk Teeth Are Not Just Temporary

The most common mistake parents make: assuming milk teeth don't need treatment because they'll fall out. Here's why that thinking costs children dearly.

  • Space holders for adult teeth — early loss causes crowding and misalignment requiring orthodontic treatment
  • Speech development — teeth shape how sounds are formed; damage affects language
  • Nutrition & eating — tooth pain means eating less, eating poorly during critical growth years
  • Confidence & social well-being — visible decay causes self-consciousness and withdrawal
  • Infection risk — untreated decay spreads to gum, jaw, and can damage the permanent tooth below
