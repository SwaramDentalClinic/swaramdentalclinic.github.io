What are Clear Aligners?

Straighter Teeth Without Anyone Knowing

Clear aligners are a series of custom-made, transparent plastic trays that gradually move your teeth into perfect alignment — without metal braces, brackets, or wires. Each set of aligners is worn for approximately 1–2 weeks before progressing to the next, gently and precisely shifting teeth toward the planned final position.

At Swaram Dental Clinic in Hinjewadi, Dr. Swati Shivane provides comprehensive clear aligner treatment using digital scanning and 3D smile simulation — so you see your straightened smile on screen before a single tray is made. No guesswork. No surprises. Just a clear plan you've already approved.

Clear aligners are the preferred choice for working professionals in Hinjewadi's IT sector who need to maintain a polished appearance throughout treatment. They're also ideal for teens and adults who want orthodontic treatment without the visibility and discomfort of traditional braces.

What We Treat

Orthodontic Issues Clear Aligners Can Correct

Clear aligners at Swaram Dental Clinic, Hinjewadi are used to treat a wide range of alignment issues. Dr. Swati Shivane will assess your case and confirm whether aligners are the right treatment for you.

  • Crowded teeth — teeth overlapping or bunched together due to insufficient jaw space
  • Gaps between teeth — spacing or diastema that creates an uneven smile
  • Overbite — upper front teeth extending too far over lower teeth
  • Underbite — lower teeth sitting in front of upper teeth
  • Crossbite — upper and lower teeth not meeting correctly when biting
  • Open bite — upper and lower front teeth don't meet when the mouth is closed
  • Misaligned midlines — upper and lower teeth centrelines not matching

Step by Step

The Clear Aligner Process at Swaram Dental Clinic

From your first consultation to your final retainer — here is exactly what to expect at every stage of treatment.

  1. 01

    Consultation & Digital Scan

    Dr. Swati Shivane examines your teeth, bite, and jaw, and takes a digital 3D scan — no messy impressions. Clinical photographs and X-rays are taken to fully map your orthodontic situation.

  2. 02

    3D Smile Simulation

    Your digital scan is used to create a complete 3D animation of how your teeth will move — tray by tray — from your current position to your final aligned smile. You see the end result before any aligner is made.

  3. 03

    Custom Aligners Manufactured

    Once you approve the treatment plan, your complete series of custom aligners is precision-manufactured from medical-grade clear plastic — fitted exactly to the 3D map of your teeth.

  4. 04

    Wear Your Aligners

    You wear each set of aligners for 1–2 weeks, 20–22 hours per day, removing them only to eat, drink, and clean your teeth. Each new set gently advances tooth movement toward the final position.

  5. 05

    Progress Reviews Every 6–8 Weeks

    Dr. Swati Shivane monitors your progress at regular appointments to confirm teeth are tracking correctly and to provide your next sets of aligners. Fewer visits than traditional braces.

  6. 06

    Retainer & Final Result

    Once alignment is complete, a retainer is fitted to hold your teeth in their new position. Retainer wear at night preserves your straight smile long-term. Your transformation is complete.

Care & Maintenance

How to Care for Your Clear Aligners

Daily Aligner Care

  • Wear aligners 20–22 hours per day for best results — remove only for eating, drinking (anything except water), and cleaning teeth
  • Rinse aligners every time you remove them — prevents bacteria buildup and keeps them clear
  • Clean aligners with a soft-bristle toothbrush and mild soap or aligner cleaning crystals — never hot water (distorts plastic)
  • Brush and floss teeth before reinserting aligners — especially after meals
  • Store aligners in their case when not in use — never wrap in a tissue or leave loose (easy to discard accidentally)

Long-Term Retention

  • Wear your post-treatment retainer every night — teeth naturally drift back without retention, regardless of how well treatment went
  • Attend 6-monthly dental check-ups at Swaram Dental Clinic to monitor alignment and retainer fit
  • Replace retainers as directed — typically every 1–2 years depending on wear
  • Contact Dr. Swati immediately if a retainer breaks or becomes uncomfortable — don't wait for your next scheduled visit
